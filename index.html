Our Mission

We build AI-powered tools that make Georgia Tech students' lives better. By harnessing the power of machine learning and artificial intelligence, we're creating practical applications that solve real problems for our fellow students.

GT²AI provides a platform for students to gain hands-on experience developing and launching AI products that have real users and impact.
